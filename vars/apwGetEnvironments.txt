<p>Get list of environments. Reuse JIRA Global Environment Variable and for full protocol, consult jira global variable</p>
<p></p>
<dl>
    <dt>application</dt><dd>Environment Application name. (Optional)</dd>
    <dt>category</dt><dd>Environment Category name. (Optional)</dd>
</dl>
<p>Usage example:</p>
<pre><code>
apwGetEnvironments: application:'eCommerce', category:'Dev'
</code></pre>
