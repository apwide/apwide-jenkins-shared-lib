<p>Update Apwide Golive application</p>
<p></p>
<dl>
    <dt>application</dt><dd>Application name. (if null, read from env.APW_APPLICATION)</dd>
    <dt>body</dt><dd>Application payload use to push data on creation.</dd>
</dl>
<br/>
<p>Usage example:</p>
<pre><code>
apwUpdateApplication application:'eCommerce' [
    versionPrefix: 'ECOM',
    mappedProjectId: 1
]
</code></pre>
